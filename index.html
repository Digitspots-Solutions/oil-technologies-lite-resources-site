About Us

Max Kings HSE Solutions Limited (also known as Max Kings Energy Safety Co) is a premier HSE services provider dedicated to ensuring operational safety and environmental compliance in Nigeria's oil and gas industry. Led by experienced Field Operations Supervisors with deep industry knowledge, we deliver world-class HSE management systems tailored to the unique challenges of Nigerian energy operations.

Our team brings extensive field experience combined with international safety standards to protect your workforce, optimize operations, and ensure regulatory compliance. From pre-operational safety assessments to ongoing HSE management, we are committed to zero-incident operations across all energy sector activities.

Our HSE Services

HSE Management Systems Development

Comprehensive design and implementation of safety management frameworks aligned with Nigerian DPR regulations and international standards (ISO 45001, ISO 14001).

Safety Training & Certification

Specialized HSE training programs including fire safety, confined space entry, permit-to-work systems, and emergency response procedures for oil & gas personnel.

Risk Assessment & Hazard Analysis

Detailed HAZOP, HAZID, and risk evaluation studies for drilling operations, production facilities, and pipeline projects across Nigerian terrain.

Environmental Compliance Management

Environmental impact assessments, waste management solutions, and regulatory compliance support for oil & gas operations in Nigeria.

Safety Audits & Inspections

Independent safety audits, equipment inspections, and compliance verification to maintain highest operational standards.

Emergency Response Planning

Development of site-specific emergency response plans, oil spill response procedures, and crisis management protocols.

Field Operations Supervision

Experienced field supervision ensuring real-time safety compliance during drilling, completion, and production operations.
